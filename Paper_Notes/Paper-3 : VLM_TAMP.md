# Guiding long horizon task and motion planning with vision language models

**Author :** Zhutian Yang

**Source :** arXiv

---

## Problems :

- Traditional task and motion planners generate multiple paths which may or maynot be feasible.
- Generating a accurate 3d map of the whole envirnment is time and compute consuming.
- Finding the right task plan and the algorithm to run is compute heavy.

---

## Objective :

By using vision language model we can check the feasibility of task plans
